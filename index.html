About Club

The Jadavpur University Science Club is one of the most dominant clubs in Jadavpur University Campus. Though named as "Science Club", the club mainly deals with Robotics. This club has participated in many robotic events, and have won awards. Now add more here..





Three Steps to Robot



There is nothing you can't do, and there is nothing you can. There is a small grey area between, and that's where we operate.

Make Structure

Use your mechanics. Find suitable parts. Assemble in the best possible way.
Your Structure is good to go!

Develop Circuit

Make the network to channel current in perfect places. Otherwise your robot won't run!

Write Code

Code is the brain of a robot. Use it well, and your robot is ready to roll.
